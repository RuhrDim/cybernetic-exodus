# Book 1: Critique and Transition
### *Cybernetic Exodus · Volume I*

> "The free development of each is the condition for the free development of all."  
> — K. Marx, *The Communist Manifesto*

---

*This book is the first part of **The Cybernetic Exodus**. It contains a Marxist analysis of the crisis of capitalism, the mechanics of transition, and the architecture of the Global Cybernetic Planner (GCP). Book 2 — [The Architecture of the Future](../book2/README.md) — examines human life inside this system. The books can be read independently, but together they form a single whole.*
