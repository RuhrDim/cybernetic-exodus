# Book 2: The Architecture of the Future
### *Cybernetic Exodus · Volume II*

> "The free development of each is the condition for the free development of all."  
> — K. Marx, *The Communist Manifesto*

---

*This book is the second part of **The Cybernetic Exodus**. The first part — [Critique and Transition](../book1/README.md) — contains a Marxist analysis of the crisis of capitalism, the mechanics of transition, and the architecture of the GCP. The basic principles of the GCP's operation are outlined in Book 1; here, we examine human life within this system. The books can be read independently, but together they form a single whole.*
